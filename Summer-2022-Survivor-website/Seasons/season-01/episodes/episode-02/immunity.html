IMMUNITY CHALLENGE
"Each tribe member will eat a beetle larvae, called butok. If there are tribe members who do not complete the task, their tribe will lose the challenge. If everyone completes the task successfully, including the extra member on the tribe that won the first immunity challenge, then both tribes will choose a member from the opposite tribe that they believe would be the slowest in eating a butok. First sudden death participant to finish their butok the fastest will win immunity for their tribe."
